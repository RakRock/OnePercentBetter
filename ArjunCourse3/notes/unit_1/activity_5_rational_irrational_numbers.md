## Quick Review Notes

### Main Idea
The number line holds **rationals** (fractions, terminating/repeating decimals) and **irrationals** (like ‚ąö2, ‚ąö3, ŌÄ). You **estimate** square roots by bracketing between perfect squares.

### Key Vocabulary
- **Irrational number:** Not equal to any `p/q`; non-repeating, non-terminating decimal.
- **Perfect square:** `1, 4, 9, 16, ‚Ä¶` ‚ÄĒ helps estimate ‚ąö.
- **Estimate:** Approximate to a given place value.

### Important Formulas / Rules

### Mini Summary
